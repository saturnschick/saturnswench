	Breakfast with the husband 🥞 

My most favorite part of being married is the slow mornings when hubby and I will share breakfast. 

With my husband everything is done in a slow relaxed manner. There is no rush, no shouting, no stressful mornings. This is such a breath of fresh air for me as an eldest daughter of a Filipino household. I am used to waking up in the morning with my siblings fighting over some chores because they'll be late to school or papa getting stressed because he couldn't find a sock, mama scolding everybody.
